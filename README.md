# VoiceShop: Hands-Free Audio Grocery Assistant 🛒🍎

VoiceShop is a personal DIY utility designed to make grocery shopping safer and more convenient by providing an interactive, audio-based checklist via a phone call.

## 🌟 The Problem & Solution
Managing a heavy shopping cart while constantly looking at a smartphone screen is cumbersome and sometimes unsafe. VoiceShop allows users to call their dedicated Twilio number and have their grocery list read to them item by item.

## 🛠 Key Features:
- **Audio Checklist:** Hear your shopping list read aloud.
- **Keypad Interaction:** Press '1' to confirm an item and move to the next.
- **Repeat Function:** Press '2' to hear the current item again.
- **Hands-Free:** Keep your eyes on the shelves and your hands on the cart.

## 🔧 Technical Stack
- **Backend:** Python (Flask)
- **API:** Twilio Programmable Voice (TwiML)
- **Deployment:** Designed for personal cloud hosting (e.g., PythonAnywhere/Heroku).

## 🔒 Privacy & Usage
This is a personal productivity tool. It only interacts with the owner's pre-saved shopping list and does not support outbound marketing or bulk messaging.
